OUR BRANDS

Discover Our Cherished Brands

We’re on a mission to create food that truly elevates your cat’s or dog’s life. Because food is not just
energy, it’s a source of joy, nutrition, and care, and we know you want to give your beloved family
member an extraordinary life. When you choose one of our brands, you know that you will get
exceptional pet food, prepared and produced with the famously high Swedish quality standards.

DOGGY | DOGGY PROFESSIONAL | DOGGY DELIKATESS

Doggy is Sweden’s leading dog food and offers a balanced and
irresistible diet. Our extensive assortment of dry and wet formulas
has been carefully curated with handpicked ingredients, catering to
dogs of every size and life phase. And don’t miss out on exploring
our delectable range of dog treats – all naturally formulated,
without a trace of added sugar

MJAU | MJAU PLUS | MJAU NOGA UTVALT

Mjau is food created for even the most picky eaters among cats.
With our range of dry and wet formulas, you can be sure to find just
the one that will make your cat purr. We’ve handpicked the finest
ingredients, including premium chicken and fish, and ensured
they’re all-natural, with no additives. Explore our array of cat treats
too, perfect for those occasions when you want to give your
beloved feline friend an extra touch of indulgence.

BOZITA | BOZITA ROBUR

Bozita is our top-tier, super-premium food of the utmost quality,
prepared with prime ingredients. Bozita is especially tailored
for active and high-performance cats and dogs that demand additional
energy and nourishment. Provide your darling pet with what they
deserve, and let them enjoy the finest that Sweden has to offer!
